About Me

I'm a Materials Science and Engineering student with a passion for building and creating. When I'm not in the lab or classroom, you'll find me on the trails mountain biking, exploring new terrain and pushing my limits.

This portfolio showcases a variety of projects I've completed for both fun and school, ranging from motorized vehicles to precision machining and experimental coatings. Each project reflects my curiosity and hands-on approach to engineering challenges.

Featured Work

Motorized Couch

Transformed a standard couch into a fully functional motorized vehicle capable of cruising around campus. This project involved custom fabrication of the frame, integration of an electric motor system, and development of a steering mechanism. The result is a comfortable, mobile seating solution that turns heads and challenges conventional transportation design.

Machined Bike Components

Designed and precision-machined custom components for my mountain bike using CNC milling and manual lathe work. This project involved CAD modeling, material selection for optimal strength-to-weight ratios, and careful machining to achieve tight tolerances. Components include a custom stem, handlebar spacers, and seat post collar machined from aluminum alloy.

Mountain Bike Suspension Telemetry

Developed a wireless telemetry system to capture real-time suspension data during mountain bike rides. The system uses linear potentiometers and accelerometers to measure fork and shock travel, along with a microcontroller for data logging. This allows for detailed analysis of suspension performance, helping optimize setup for different trail conditions and riding styles.

Grip Enhancing Wrench Coating

Developed and tested an experimental coating to improve grip and ergonomics of hand tools. The coating combines polymer materials with micro-textured particles to increase friction while maintaining durability. This materials science project involved synthesis of various formulations, application techniques, and comprehensive testing for wear resistance, chemical compatibility, and grip performance under different conditions.
